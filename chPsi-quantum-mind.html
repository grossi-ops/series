II.5 Astrophysical Consequence — Neutron Stars

In degenerate matter — neutron stars, white dwarfs, the quark-gluon plasma — the Pauli exclusion principle creates long-range correlations between fermion positions. The Zitterbewegung phases are not independent. The cancellation is incomplete.
