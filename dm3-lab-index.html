The dm³ System at a Glance

The ODE lives on (M, ξ) = (ℝ³, ker α) in cylindrical coordinates, with ε = 2 throughout:   ṙ = r(1−r²) + ε(r−1)e−z,   θ̇ = 1,   ż = r² − ε(r−1)²e−z The limit set is a helix — the Reeb orbit Γ on the unit cylinder r = 1. Non-integrability of ξ forces any planar periodic limit to become helical in 3D.
