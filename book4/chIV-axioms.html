The Seven Axioms

Os Sete Axiomas — The irreducible foundations of Generative Temporal Contact Theory

Seven axioms on a contact manifold $M$ — the minimal irreducible set from which the entire GTCT structure follows: the twelve-phase dimensional field, the four base operators $C$, $K$, $F$, $U$, the three global invariants $I_1$, $I_2$, $I_3$, and the Banach fixed point $\xstar$. All structural constants ($g_{33} = 33$, $\varepsilon^* = 1/3$, $T^* = 2\pi$) are machine-verified in Lean 4 (AXLE, 0 sorry on structural claims).

The Seven Axioms · Os Sete Axiomas
A1 Contact Manifold · Variedade de Contato ✓ Lean 4
$(M, \alpha)$ is a smooth oriented contact manifold of dimension $2n+1$, with contact form $\alpha$ satisfying $\alpha \wedge (d\alpha)^n \neq 0$ everywhere on $M$.
$(M, \alpha)$ é uma variedade de contato suave orientada de dimensão $2n+1$, com forma de contato $\alpha$ satisfazendo $\alpha \wedge (d\alpha)^n \neq 0$ em todo ponto.
α ∧ (dα)ⁿ ≠ 0 everywhere on M
A2 Dimensional Field · Campo Dimensional ✓ Lean 4
There exists a smooth map $\Delta : M \to \mathbb{R}^{12}$, $\Delta(x) = (\delta_1(x), \ldots, \delta_{12}(x))$, with $\|\Delta(x)\| > 0$ for all $x \in M$. The component functions $\delta_i$ are called phase functions.
Existe uma aplicação suave $\Delta : M \to \mathbb{R}^{12}$ com $\|\Delta(x)\| > 0$ para todo $x \in M$. As funções componentes $\delta_i$ são chamadas funções de fase.
Δ : M → ℝ¹², ‖Δ(x)‖ > 0 for all x ∈ M
A3 Temporal Field · Campo Temporal ✓ Lean 4
A non-vanishing vector field $T$ on $M$ with flow $\varphi_t$ satisfying $\varphi_t^* \alpha = e^{f(t)} \alpha$ for some smooth function $f$. The field $T$ plays the role of the Reeb vector field modulo the conformal factor $e^{f(t)}$.
Campo vetorial $T$ não-nulo em $M$ com fluxo $\varphi_t$ satisfazendo $\varphi_t^* \alpha = e^{f(t)} \alpha$ para alguma função suave $f$.
φ_t* α = e^{f(t)} α, T ≠ 0
A4 Triad of Invariants · Tríade de Invariantes ✓ Lean 4
Three global invariants govern the cycle: $I_1$ (Orthogonality) — consecutive phase vectors are orthogonal in $\mathbb{R}^{12}$; $I_2$ (Nilpotency) — critical exponent $\varepsilon^* = 1/3$; $I_3$ (Spectral Collapse) — eigenvalue spectrum collapses at resonance.
Três invariantes globais: $I_1$ (Ortogonalidade); $I_2$ (Nilpotência) — $\varepsilon^* = 1/3$; $I_3$ (Colapso Espectral) — espectro de autovalores colapsa na ressonância.
I₁: ⟨δᵢ, δᵢ₊₁⟩ = 0 | I₂: ε* = 1/3 | I₃: spectral collapse
A5 Twelve-Phase Cycle · Ciclo de Doze Fases ✓ Lean 4
The phase functions satisfy $\delta_{i+12}(x) = \delta_i(x)$ for all $i \in \mathbb{Z}$ and all $x \in M$. The dominant phase of $x$ is $i^*(x) = \arg\max_i \delta_i(x)$; we write $x \in D_{i^*(x)}$. The twelve dimensional phases $D_1, \ldots, D_{12}$ partition $M$ generically.
As funções de fase satisfazem $\delta_{i+12}(x) = \delta_i(x)$ para todo $i \in \mathbb{Z}$ e todo $x \in M$. A fase dominante de $x$ é $i^*(x) = \arg\max_i \delta_i(x)$.
δ_{i+12}(x) = δᵢ(x) for all i ∈ ℤ, x ∈ M
A6 Phase–Operator Bijection · Bijeção Fase–Operador ✓ Lean 4
There exists a bijection $\Phi : \{D_1, \ldots, D_{12}\} \to \{O_1, \ldots, O_{12}\}$ such that each operator $O_i = \Phi(D_i)$ maps $D_i$ into $D_{i+1 \bmod 12}$. (Uniqueness is proved in Theorem 4.1; this axiom asserts existence.)
Existe bijeção $\Phi$ tal que $O_i = \Phi(D_i)$ leva $D_i$ em $D_{i+1 \bmod 12}$. (Unicidade provada no Teorema 4.1; este axioma afirma existência.)
Φ(Dᵢ) = Oᵢ, Oᵢ(Dᵢ) ⊆ D_{i+1 mod 12}
A7 Cycle Map Contraction · Contração do Mapa do Ciclo ✓ Lean 4
The cycle map $R = O_{12} \circ \cdots \circ O_1$ generates a sequence $x_0, x_{12}, x_{24}, \ldots$ converging to a unique fixed point $\xstar$. The structural constants are $g_{33} = 33$ and $g_{64} = 64 = 2^6$.
O mapa do ciclo $R = O_{12} \circ \cdots \circ O_1$ gera sequência convergindo a ponto fixo único $\xstar$. Constantes estruturais: $g_{33} = 33$ e $g_{64} = 64 = 2^6$.
R = O₁₂ ∘ ⋯ ∘ O₁, x_{12k} → x*, g₃₃ = 33, g₆₄ = 64
