Plainly, once

What follows is not abstract. The operator chain G = U ∘ F ∘ K ∘ C appears in DNA replication, RNA transcription, ribosomal translation, microtubule assembly, immune selection, cardiac rhythms, and neural attractor networks. Six biological domains, twelve new chapters. The mathematics is not imposed on biology — it was always there.
